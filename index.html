About SheMovesSafe 🛡️

Our mission is to create a safer world for women by providing intelligent, real-time navigation that prioritizes safety over speed — not just the fastest route, but the safest one.

🗺️

3-Route Navigation

Three color-coded routes — Green (Safest), Yellow (Moderate), Red (Unsafe) — displayed simultaneously on the map.

🔍

Smart Analysis

AI-powered risk scoring using 20,000+ real crime and safety records across India.

🚨

SOS Integration

One-tap emergency alerts with GPS location, priority contact calling, and auto audio recording.

📢

Community Reports

Real-time safety feedback from the community that dynamically updates route scores.

⏱️

Check-In Timer

Set a countdown timer — if you don't check in, SOS triggers automatically.

🎙️

Evidence Vault

Voice recordings captured during SOS are saved securely for later review.
